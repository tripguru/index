About Us

User Persona

A snapshot of our diverse customer base and their travel preferences.
Our customers are diverse and globally connected, ranging from independent travelers seeking flexibility and freedom to families and groups looking for curated travel experiences. Many of our users are international travelers planning trips to iconic destinations like Las Vegas, the Grand Canyon, and other Southwest landmarks. They value seamless travel planning, competitive pricing, and unique travel opportunities such as customized tours, shuttle networks, and luxury transportation.

Our users often prefer booking through our customer-facing platform for its ease of use and the ability to combine flights, hotels, tours, and event tickets in one itinerary. Additionally, businesses and travel producers rely on our service management platform to create bespoke travel solutions, showcasing our strong reputation in the industry. As travel preferences evolve, our customer base increasingly demands independence, flexibility, and access to innovative travel solutions that cater to their specific needs.

Growth History

The story of how National Park Express has grown and evolved over the years.
National Park Express is part of CHD, Inc., a privately held company founded in 2000. With over 7 million customers served, CHD, Inc. started as an inbound tour company for travelers to Las Vegas and the Grand Canyon. Over time, the company expanded to include meeting and incentive services, FIT and group itineraries, shuttle bus services, bespoke travel arrangements, and proprietary reservation platforms for international travel producers.
  • 2000 - Founded

    CHD, Inc. was founded in 2000 as an inbound tour company for travelers to Las Vegas and the Grand Canyon.

    Over 7 million customers served since inception.
  • 2005 - Service Expansion

    Added meeting and incentive services, as well as FIT and group itineraries, catering to more diverse travel needs.

  • 2010 - Shuttle Bus Network

    Launched a shuttle bus network to connect destinations, providing travelers with more freedom and flexibility in their itineraries.

  • Present Day

    Operates a fleet of over 30 vehicles, including full-size motor coaches, mini-buses, and sprinters. Based in Las Vegas, the headquarters includes offices, full-service maintenance bays, and a large coach yard, with services extending to other transportation companies in the region.

Our Brands

National Park Express

National Park Express offers day trips and a shuttle network, connecting travelers between Las Vegas, Zion National Park, Bryce Canyon, Antelope Canyon, and other destinations. It provides flexible itineraries for transportation, accommodations, tours, and attractions throughout the Southwest United States.

Travel USA Express

Travel USA Express connects travelers with accommodations, tours, and attractions across the USA. Travelers can book single items or use the Itinerary Builder to create multi-day experiences, promoting seamless and customizable travel planning.

Boulder City Express

Boulder City Express offers tours, transportation, and activities in Boulder City, just 45 minutes from the Las Vegas Strip. Visitors can explore Hoover Dam, the Historic District, antique shops, and the famous art walk with bronze sculptures.

Canyon Coach Lines

Canyon Coach Lines provides charter services with luxury SUVs, Sprinter Vans, and Motor Coaches for groups of all sizes. Serving Las Vegas and the Southwest United States, it offers the newest fleet for premium transportation needs.
